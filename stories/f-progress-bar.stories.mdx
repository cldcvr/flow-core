# f-progress-bar

<br />
<br />

## Overview

<f-divider></f-divider>

Progress bar is a visual representation of completion of a specific process.

<br />

## variant

<f-divider></f-divider>

Variants are visual representations of progress bar. A progress bar can be round or block.

<br />
